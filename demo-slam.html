Present a Demo Slam

Raise your profile in the Google training community and get completely free entry by presenting at one of our summits or cloud camps.

At the end of the first day of a summit we invite and encourage everybody to showcase a tip or trick in 2 minutes or less! It could be something of your own, or an excellent tip you've picked up at the summit. Always practice your demo to help the slams run smoothly!

They should be quick, simple and engaging. Look for something that can more generically be used. It can be geeky or just plain useful. Examples:

  • Useful Chrome Extension
  • Useful 'Lab' or Add On
  • Unique uses for apps like Drawings or Sheets
