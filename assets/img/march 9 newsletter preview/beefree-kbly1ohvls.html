Upcoming Events

Dear Educator,

 

In this week’s newsletter, we are featuring a contributed article from AppsEvents presenter, Dr. Larverne Hill, where she shares her list of what she calls “Chrome Gnomes”---little hidden gems in the Chrome browser you may not know exists or features you are not utilizing at full potential.

 

One of our favorite part about attending a Google PD (regardless if your a speaker, attendee, or organizer) are those moments where you would say, "why didn't I think of that?". Dr. Hill's blog post gives you a similar feeling so make sure to check it out.

 

Also, we are happy to have kicked off March with three Google PD events on the same weekend! An internal staff G Suite training in Rome, Google Summit in Berlin, and very successful first event in Latin America (Lima, Peru) where we offered English and Spanish sessions of our Google Educator and Certified Admin preparation courses.

 

For those who missed the first Peru event, we will be offering another round of Google certifications bootcamps this June followed by a 2-day Summit.

 

Apart from our standardized Bootcamps and Summit, we can also offer customised PD for your staff and teachers. Schedule a call with one of our Directors and we will be happy to provide you with more information and the best advise on how you can build a rock-solid Google Community at your school. Register your interest here!

 

You can also opt to send a few staff to any of our upcoming events. Early Bird and Super Early Bird promo are ongoing.
