In addition to our core team of speakers we welcome others to submit session proposals for any summit. Speakers must be passionate and knowledgeable. If a summit is scheduled in your area and you are already a G Suite or edtech expert, presenting is a great opportunity. It allows you to raise your profile in Google for Education and edtech circles by sharing your talents and skills with a broad audience of educators from around the world.

Once your session is reviewed and accepted your session(s) and speaker status will be promoted on social media to help you gain more recognition on a global platform.

Benefits

If your session is selected, you will receive:

  • Waived registration fee to the entire summit and activities ($300 value)
  • Your details included on the summit website in the speakers section
  • Recognition throughout Google for Education communities
  • Experience presenting outside your school (especially helpful if you are planning to become a Google for Education Certified Trainer)
  • The AppsEvent Summit Speaker badge to use on your website, blog or email signature
  • Speakers who've presented at three or more summits are entitled to use the Featured Speaker badge
